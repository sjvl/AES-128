Ciphway
Art and code by @sjvl for SHL n STid.

AES-128 algorithm is used in Stid's DESFire® boxes to certify identities.

With this piece, artist reveals all the steps involved in the AES-128 encryption process.

The 16 bits of the cipher are used to create (x,y) coordinates of 8 points.

These points are interpolated into a spline on which circles are drawn, to plot a “path between two doors”, evoking the STid logotype and generating a unique signature.
